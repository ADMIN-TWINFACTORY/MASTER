Where It Takes Form Onde Ganha Forma

Sensing[1]

Biomechanical sensing technologies capture motion, force and neuromuscular signals, creating the data foundation for modeling human movement. Tecnologias de sensorização biomecânica captam movimento, força e sinais neuromusculares, criando a base de dados para modelar o movimento humano.

Actuation[2]

Electromechanical actuation systems interact collaboratively with human movement, modulating force and resistance in real time to guide, adapt and optimize movement dynamics. Sistemas de atuação eletromecânica interagem de forma colaborativa com o movimento humano, modulando força e resistência em tempo real para orientar, adaptar e otimizar a dinâmica do movimento.

Modeling[3]

Dynamic 3D biomechanical modeling transforms movement data into computational representations of the human body, enabling predictive models of motor behavior. Modelação biomecânica dinâmica em 3D transforma dados de movimento em representações computacionais do corpo humano, permitindo a construção de modelos preditivos do comportamento motor.

Intelligence[4]

Artificial intelligence learns movement patterns, anticipates behaviors and optimizes motor performance in real time. Sistemas de inteligência artificial aprendem padrões de movimento, antecipam comportamentos e otimizam a performance motora em tempo real.

In Industry 4.0, value is born from orchestration — not from isolated technologies.

The same principle applies here.

At Twin Factory, six disciplines converge into a single stack. The output is not a list of features — it is the Body Operating System [BoS]: a system that reads, models, simulates and acts on human movement.

HARD LAYER — BODY
IoB

INTERNET OF BODY

Movement has never been truly measurable — until sensors became precise enough, connected enough and invisible enough to live on the body. IoB is the evolution of IoT: not things connected, but the human body itself — readable, in real time, anywhere.

NANO

NANOTECHNOLOGY

Conventional sensors sit on the body. Nanotechnology becomes the body — fibres engineered at molecular level that sense and stimulate neuromuscular activity without interrupting movement. The textile is the sensor. The skin is the interface.

COBOT

COLLABORATIVE ROBOT

Not a robot that replaces human movement — a system that works with it. The Cobot doesn't impose, it collaborates: generating load, applying FES stimulation, adjusting resistance in real time. It doesn't do the movement for you. It does it with you — and makes it better.

SOFT LAYER — DIGITAL
AI

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

The human body is too complex and too individual for fixed protocols. AI runs millions of simulations to find the optimal intervention — calibrated for your body, at the exact instant of each movement. It anticipates injury, optimises performance and personalises recovery at a scale no human expert can match.

DT

DIGITAL TWIN

Not a static model — a living bridge. The Digital Twin mediates the continuous loop between the physical and the digital: the real body feeds the virtual model, the virtual model informs the real intervention, the intervention changes the body, and the body feeds the model again. Two worlds in constant dialogue.

XR

EXTENDED REALITY

Data alone doesn't change behaviour. Extended Reality translates invisible biomechanical information into experiences the body can understand, respond to and learn from — making the Digital Twin visible, tangible and actionable in real time.
