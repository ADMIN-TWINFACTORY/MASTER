Technology Driven.
Driving Technology.

Na Twin Factory não apenas aplicamos tecnologias emergentes — nós as empurramos além dos seus limites conhecidos. A criatividade e o pensamento não convencional nos levam a usar a tecnologia de maneiras que nem seus próprios criadores previram. CORE TECHNOLOGIES

The Stack.

Aproveitamos as tecnologias habilitadoras mais avançadas da Indústria 4.0 para criar produtos na interseção dos mundos digital e físico — o que chamamos de Fábrica de Gêmeos Digitais.

IoT

INTERNET OF THINGS

Connected sensors capturing real-time biodata from the human body in motion, anywhere in the world.

AI

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Predictive analytics and biomechanical intelligence that anticipates injury, optimises performance and personalises recovery.

XR

EXTENDED REALITY

Immersive interfaces and real-time 3D avatars that visualise the body's digital twin in action.

DT

DIGITAL TWINS

Virtual replicas enabling real-time monitoring, simulation and optimisation of human movement.

5G

CONNECTIVITY

Ultra-low latency wireless communication enabling real-time control and data transfer anywhere.

E·T

E-TEXTILE / NANOTECH

Nanotechnology embedded into fibres creates an intelligent second skin that reads and stimulates muscle activity.

THE FOUNDER
ANDRE SERNAGLIA
FOUNDER & CEO

ANDRE
SERNAGLIA

FOUNDER & CEO · TWIN FACTORY

Passionate enthusiast of science, technology and sports — with a special love for mountain biking and snowboarding. André embraces life as an adventure, driven by curiosity and a commitment to innovation.

With deep experience in Industry 4.0, where precision and data are essential, he asked: "Why not use these technologies to transform motor health?" Frustrated by the lack of objectivity in physical therapy after his own injury recovery, he founded Twin Factory to bridge that gap.

"This imprecision left me frustrated and convinced that there was significant room for improvement."
